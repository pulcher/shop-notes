# Resin Recipe — Huskers Red/Black/White Resin Pour Recipe

**Date:** 2026-05-12  
**Batch ID:** 1698ca91-759b-4cd6-be78-47c8ad3aee1c  
**Purpose:** A weight‑based, repeatable recipe for casting high‑contrast Huskers‑themed pen blanks in 3/4" ID vertical PVC tubes using Alumilite Clear Slow and Eye Candy pigments.

---

## Resin Volume (3/4" ID, 6" tubes)
- **48–52 g** total resin per blank  
- For **4 blanks**: **200 g total resin**
  - 100 g Part A  
  - 100 g Part B  

Split into **three equal color cups**:
- ~66 g Red  
- ~66 g Black  
- ~66 g White  

---

## Pigment Weights
### Red — Senshi Red
- **0.10–0.13 g**

### Black — Ronin Black
- **0.08–0.10 g**

### White — Shiro White
- **0.15–0.18 g**

### Optional — Ghost Interference Red
- **0.02 g** added to white cup

---

## Temperature Targets
- Warm resin to **95–100°F**
- Pre‑warm PVC tubes to **90–100°F**

---

## Timing for Swirls
- Wait **8–12 minutes** after mixing  
- Resin should reach **warm honey** viscosity  
- Test: resin falls in a slow ribbon from stir stick

---

## Pour Order
1. **White** down one side  
2. **Black** down the opposite side  
3. **Red** through the center  

Do **not** stir inside the tube.

---

## Pressure Pot
- **50–60 PSI**  
- Hold for **2–3 hours**  
- Full cure: **12–24 hours**

---

## Post‑Cure
- Push blanks out with a dowel  
- Trim ends square  
- Let rest **24 hours** before drilling

---

## Notes
- Keep black pigment lighter to avoid muddying  
- White needs more pigment for opacity  
- Red last ensures dominant streaks
